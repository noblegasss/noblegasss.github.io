Tumor and CellLine Transcriptomes Alignment

Utilizing Deep Neural Networks and Domain Adversarial Training to bridge the gap between in vitro models and clinical data.

Key Problem: Cell lines often drift from primary tumor biology. Our model corrects for this systematic bias.

Architecture

Mathematical Formulation

We minimize the following objective function combining classification loss and domain confusion loss:

$$ L = L_{task} - \lambda L_{domain} $$

  • $L_{task}$: Cross-entropy loss for the biological task (e.g., drug response).
  • $L_{domain}$: Adversarial loss to ensure feature invariance.
